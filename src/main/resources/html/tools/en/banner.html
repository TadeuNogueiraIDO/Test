What is it for?
This tool allows you to add fixed image banners to your Ido. With this feature it is possible to display a promotion, an ad or an image of a product or service. It is still possible to direct the user, through the click, to Whatsapp, email, external link or just maximize the image.
How to use it?
1- Add a tool to your Ido and select ”Image Banner”;

2- Enter a title for your banner. It will not appear to the user, it will only serve for organization;

3- Select one of the options for when the user clicks on the banner:
  • No action: there will be no click on the banner;
  • Open Whatsapp, then fill in the phone number on which you will respond on Whatsapp;
  • Open email: then enter the referring email;
  • Go to external link: Enter the URL of the website or social network;
  • Maximize Image: The banner will open in full screen;

4- Click on ”Upload” and select an image from your device.
