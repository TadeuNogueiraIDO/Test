What is it for?
Custom domain allows you to use your own domain for your website. Example:
No custom domain www.idolink.com/yoursite
With custom domain www.yoursite.com
How to use it?
1- Add your domain in the requested field and click continue;

2 - Enter the data below in the Zone and DNS editor of your domain:
  • Name *.dominio.com.br
  • CNAME user.idolink.com
Changes to CNAME records can take up to 72 hours to take effect, but the process is usually quick.
Click finish and wait for the settings to be validated.
