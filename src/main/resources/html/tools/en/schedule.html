What is it for?
Scheduling allows you to offer a service to your clients, showing them the professionals available, the services offered and the days and times available for performing the service.
You can even sync your Google Workspace or gmail account with IdoLink Scheduling and make sure you don't compromise any time on your schedule.
How to use it?
1 - In tools, select Scheduling

2 - Fill in the required presentation fields:
  • Description (optional);
  • Cover image;
  • Display floating shortcut (an icon will be visible floating on your site for the customer to access quickly).

Fill in the required settings fields:
  • Add google account to sync calendar (optional);
  • Inform when customers can make the appointment ;
    • Indefinitely
    • Date range
    • Limit by day
    • Duration of each call
    • Register of attendants informing the service commission
    • Register the agenda
    • Payment Methods
    • Add services (when adding services, if you have already registered agents, you can select which agents offer the service.

      • Finish by clicking Save.
