What is it for?
This tool helps you have your contact information available in buttons with intuitive icons and quick access to the your audience.
With this feature, it is possible to provide telephone, SMS, E-mail, Whatsapp, Telegram, and as many addresses as you need.
How to use it?
1- Add a tool to your Ido and select ”Contacts”;

2- Activate the contacts you think are important for your page:
  • Phone: enter the phone number on which you will receive calls;
  • SMS: enter the phone number on which you will receive messages;
  • Email: enter your minisite or personal email;
  • Whatsapp: Enter the phone number where you will respond on Whatsapp;
  • Telegram: Enter the phone number where you will respond on Telegram;
  • Address: Enter one or more addresses if necessary.
    Ex: if your Ido goes to restaurants, you can have branches in different locations.
