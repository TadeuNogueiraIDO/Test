What is it for?
FAQ is an acronym for the English language that comes from the expression “Frequently Asked Questions”, that is, Frequently Answered Questions, or simply Frequently Asked Questions.
With this tool it is possible to register questions and answers that your followers or users always ask. This creates a channel of communication, self-service and facilitates the resolution of doubts and problems.
How to use it?
1- Add a tool to your Ido and select ”FAQ”;

2- Fill in the information on the page:
  • Title: Enter a title for the button (Ex: FAQ, Frequently Asked Questions, Ask your questions here);
  • Subtitle: A catchy phrase that interests your audience (optional);
  • Icon: Choose whether you want no icon, Gallery Ido (we have some icons available for you in our gallery) or upload directly from your device;

3- Click on the ”Add question and answer” button and fill in the information on the following screen:
  • Question: Create a question that you feel is asked frequently;
  • Answer: Write an answer to the question clearly and useful for similar questions. You can format as you wish, add images and links within the text.
Upon saving, this question + answer group will become a card on the main screen which you can open and close to view the content. You can also click delete or edit.
