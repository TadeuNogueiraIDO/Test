What is it for?
The link button is a tool widely used to list websites, blogs, social networks or any page that you need quick access to your audience. With this feature, you can create a tree of links to concentrate all your pages and make your Ido address available anywhere.
How to use it?
1- Add a tool to your Ido and select ”Link button”;
2- Fill in the information on the page:
  • Title: the name of your network or website;
  • Subtitle: A catchy phrase that interests your audience;
  • Link: address of your page (usually starts with www or http);
  • Icon: No icon, Social (select the logos of the most used social networks), Ido Gallery (we have some icons available for you in our gallery) or upload directly from your device (it can be a photo, logo, icon or any image).
3- You can still activate the animation of the button so that it has a greater prominence among the others.
