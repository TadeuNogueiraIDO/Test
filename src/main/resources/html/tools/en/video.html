What is it for?
With this tool, you can index videos from Youtube, Vimeo or TikTok to be streamed right there on your gone.
It is an essential tool for anyone who wants to present a tutorial, make a more detailed disclosure of products and services or even show featured videos of a channel!
How to use it?
1- Add a tool to your Ido and select “Video”;

2- Fill in the information:
  • Title: Give your video a title. It will only appear in your structure area to help with organization.
  • Link: Type or copy and paste the URL (address) of the video from platforms like Youtube, Vimeo or TikTok.
NOTE: For the public, neither the title nor the link will appear, only the thumbnail of the video with the option to play.
