What is it for?
This tool allows you to present your establishment's opening hours or even your working hours . You can also create an alternate time for a specific date or period, such as holidays or vacations.
How to use it?
1- Add a tool to your Ido and select ”Operating hours”;

2- Enter a title for the button. Ex: Opening hours, Access our hours now;

3-Select if you want the button without icon, from Ido Gallery (we have some icons available for you in our gallery) or upload from your device (it can be a photo, icon or any image).

4- Select the time:
  • To be defined: you can manually set the schedules, activating the days of the week and adding 2 or 4 schedules for each day;
  • 24 hours: with this option enabled, you claim that you are working all the time, every day;
5- If necessary, you can create an alternate time for a specific date or period. It will temporarily overwrite the previously defined one and will expire once finished.
You can also create an alert message to inform the user of this alternate time. It will appear highlighted, just above the other information.
